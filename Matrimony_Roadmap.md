# Matrimony Project Roadmap: Future Enhancements

Based on the current project assessment, here are the proposed feature enhancements categorized by impact and complexity. These features are standard for modern, trust-driven matrimony platforms.

## 🛡️ Phase 1: Trust & Verification (High Priority)
Building trust is the core of any matrimony site. Users need to feel safe and verified.

### 1. Enhanced Trust Score Dashboard
Currently, the trust score is a simple client-side calculation.
- **Dynamic Scoring**: Detailed points for Mobile (+10%), Email (+10%), KYC ID (+50%), Social Links (+15%), and Professional Verification (+15%).
- **Visual Breakdown**: A dedicated section explaining *exactly* what steps a user can take to reach 100%.

### 2. Social Media Verification
Integrate social login/verification (LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram) without displaying the profiles, purely for trust validation.

### 3. Detailed Profile Audit
An automated system that flags profiles with "Low Quality" bio or suspicious photos for manual admin review.

---

## 🚀 Phase 2: Engagement & Matchmaking
Features to keep users coming back and provide better value.

### 4. "Daily Top Picks" Algorithm
Instead of showing all matches, feature 3-5 "Top Picks" every day based on the deterministic compatibility score and partner preferences.

### 5. Profile Completeness Wizard
A "Next Step" widget on the homepage that prompts users: *"Did you know? Verified profiles get 5x more interests. Click here to verify Aadhar."*

### 6. Success Stories Board
A public-facing page showcasing couples who found matches on your platform. This is the single most effective marketing/trust tool.

---

## 💎 Phase 3: Monetization & Premium Experience
Turning the platform into a sustainable business.

### 7. Premium Membership Tiers
- **Silver/Gold/Diamond Plans**:
    - **Benefits**: View direct contact info, Highlighted profiles in search results, Messaging without waiting for interest acceptance.
    - **UI**: Premium crown badges and distinct card borders.

### 8. Introduction Messages
Currently, "Express Interest" is a single button. Adding a small "Hi, I liked your profile because..." text field significantly increases the chance of acceptance.

---

## 🕉️ Phase 4: Cultural & Niche Features
Specialized features for the regional matrimony market.

### 9. Advanced Astro/Horoscope Matching
Integrating a deeper compatibility engine (e.g., Guna Milan or detailed chart comparison) for users who prioritize astrological compatibility.

### 10. Voice/Video Introductions
Allow users to record a 30-second audio or video introduction to make their profile feel more "human" and alive.

---

## 🛠️ Recommended Next Steps
1. **Implementation of Trust Score Details**: Create a dedicated "Trust Center" page.
2. **Success Stories UI**: Design a beautiful layout for curated stories.
3. **Premium Pricing Page**: Design a premium tier UI.
